## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:
- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging issues and security reports
- Releasing new versions
- Maintaining the specsmith governance integration
- Ensuring build health (`cargo check -p kairos`) and dependency health

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who demonstrate sustained, high-quality contributions may be invited to become maintainers. Reach out via [GitHub issues](https://github.com/BitConcepts/kairos/issues) or [email](mailto:info@bitconcepts.tech).
